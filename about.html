A Toronto-based artist with a passion for problem solving, creative thinking, visual communication and power leveling. He illustrates and designs, and also on occasion gets crafty. You can find him stealing free magazines to fuel his illustrations.

Rocky received his BAA in Illustration from Sheridan College Institute of Technology and Advanced Learning. He is continually on the look out for new opportunities and channels to further develop as an artist and expand his knowledge base.
